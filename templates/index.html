Salary Management

Why Use This System?

Our Employee Management System helps organizations centralize employee data, reduce paperwork, and improve decision-making.

Built with Flask (backend), SQLAlchemy (database), TailwindCSS (styling), and Jinja2 templates.

Future updates will include:

  • Full CRUD operations (Add, Edit, Delete, View)
  • Search and filter functionality
  • Role-based access control
  • Employee photo gallery
